Entertainment Tracker

Made for IOS with Swift

About the Project

Create your own virtual library collection of tv shows, movies and books. Keep track of what entertainment you have, are currently, or want to enjoy.

The library is organized by tv shows, movies and books. Add new items to your library and mark them as to do, in progress, or finished. You can add ratings and include a picture from your camera or library. For tv shows you can keep track of them by season.

Edit existing items to change the rating and progress. Then use the map button to search for local stores to purchase your entertainment from.

Search for items in your library.

If you are feeling bored and want to find something else to do then discover new activities. Using an API to retrieve the activities you can choose any type of activity or completely random.

This project was my final project for a class in college. I also created a video presentation which includes the class diagram, MVC diagram, and lessons learned. Check out the full video here
